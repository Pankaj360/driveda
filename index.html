About Us

Rivnr is a mobile app which helps the learner drivers or anyone who seek driving lessons to find qualified driving instructors for a reasonable and transparent cost.

As a learner driver, finding a good driving instructor for a reasonable price is difficult.

Unless through a referral these learner drivers have no means to confirm the expertise of their instructors.

Meanwhile, there are many skilled independent driving instructors facing difficulties in finding people who needs driving instructions. Currently, their primary mode of finding leaners are referrals, or online advertisement listing websites such as Kijiji or Craigslist.

  • Licensed Driving Instructors with driving instructor’s license.
  • Rating/feedback system to maintain a standard and quality of service.
  • Customized lessons based on learner’s needs.
  • Clear pricing to let learners know the price up front and know what price includes.
